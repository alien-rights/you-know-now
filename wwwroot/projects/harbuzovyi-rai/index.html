Overview

I was tasked with designing an e-commerce website for a Ukrainian craft cheese company to help them sell their products online. The goal was to create a user-centric design for the main page, catalog, product page, basket, and checkout pages that would provide a simple and fast ordering experience, present information logically, and focus on the shop's offerings.

As part of my Web Design Junior course, I worked under the supervision of two mentors who provided guidance and feedback throughout the process. The design process involved creating wireframes, testing them, and iterating to develop a final prototype that aligned with the client's requirements and user needs.

Original Design Assessment

Through a thorough review of the original website, I identified several key design issues that were impacting the user experience and potentially hindering sales:

  • Category visibility: Categories are hidden under "shop" menu, hindering navigation.
  • Inconsistent green labels: Used for both new products and weight, causing confusion.
  • Quick preview limitations: Minimal information and poor "Add to basket" button design.
  • Undifferentiated sections: Descriptions, ingredients, and nutrition blend together.
  • Inconsistent weight placement: Located in varying places across pages.
  • Missing product information: Ingredients and nutrition not always included, impacting informed decisions.
  • Unclear discounts: Mentions lower prices for bulk purchases but no direct option to apply.
  • Vague gift set contents: Insufficient details about included products in gift sets.

Design considerations

Based on my analysis of the original website and client requirements, I identified several key areas for improvement:

01

Lighter color scheme

To accommodate the client's request for a video, I decided to make the design of the header simple to prevent visual overload and keep the focus on key information.

02

Simplified navigation

Streamline the site structure and menu organization to facilitate easier browsing and product discovery.

03

Engaging product video

Incorporate a high-quality, informative product video in the header to showcase the unique qualities and benefits of the company's offerings.

04

Enhanced product pages

Design a standardized layout for presenting product descriptions, ingredients, and nutritional details to enhance clarity and comparability.

05

Filtering options

Implement filters for product type, availability, and other key attributes to enable efficient product search and comparison.

06

Recipe inspiration

Create a dedicated recipe section to foster community engagement, showcase product versatility, and provide value-added content for customers.

User Testing

Usability tests were conducted on a sample size of 6 people to identify major issues and pain points in the user experience on the top tasks:

  1. Finding products
  2. Adding chosen products to basket
  3. Checkout process

Adding to basket

“If you add to the basket on the main page or the catalogue, it would be helpful to show more prominent confirmation that you’ve added it to the basket.”

Checkout

“To make it more obvious for users that they have to choose something, you could make the button inactive or not show it to users until they choose delivery or payment type.”

Recipes

“It would be nice to show the recipes for the specific cheese on the product’s page.”

Key Improvements in Redesign

  • Streamlined navigation with prominent product categories and intuitive menu structure

  • Consistent layout for product pages

Conclusion

Through this project, I applied my design skills to enhance the e-commerce website. By identifying areas for improvement, considering client requirements, testing, and creating a user-focused design, I delivered a solution that streamlines the user experience and effectively showcases the company's unique products.

Key learnings include the importance of establishing a consistent visual language through color and typography styles, as well as the potential value of incorporating user-centric features such as a "Notify When in Stock" option to improve customer engagement.

Collaborating closely with stakeholders, I ensured the redesigned website aligned with their vision and communicated the quality of their products.
